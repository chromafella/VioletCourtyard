About the Band

Formed in Knoxville in 2020, Violet Courtyard blends alternative rock and indie‑pop energy. Ajay on vocals & guitars; Brennan producing, mixing, and drums; Harrison on drums & production assist. Influences range from Far Caspian to Dayglow, and the sound lands somewhere between neon nostalgia and clean modern hooks.
